Swift is a powerful and intuitive programming language developed by Apple. It's used to build apps for iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS.

## Why Swift?

Swift is designed to be safe, fast, and expressive. It eliminates entire classes of common programming errors, and its syntax is concise yet expressive.

## Your First Program

```swift
print("Hello, world!")
```

That's all it takes to get started.
