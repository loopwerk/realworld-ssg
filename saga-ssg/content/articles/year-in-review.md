# 2024 Year in Review

What a year it's been for static site generators. Let's look back at the highlights.

## New Tools

Several new SSGs emerged this year, each with their own take on the problem.

## Community Growth

The static site community continues to grow. More developers are choosing SSGs for their personal sites, documentation, and even commercial projects.

## Looking Ahead

The future looks bright. With improvements in build times, better tooling, and growing adoption, static sites are here to stay.
